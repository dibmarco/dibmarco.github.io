Sections

Sections are good for dividing topics on a webpage.

For instance, the contets between the HR tags are Sections.

<hr>
<section>Content</section>
<hr>

Each topic that I want to add a description or a note will be within one. So it's easier to find them in the future.

Using Article tags are also valid. Then you may have sections within Article tags.
